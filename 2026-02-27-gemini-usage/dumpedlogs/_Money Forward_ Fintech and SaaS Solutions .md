# you asked

What do you call Money Forward as? Like No. 1 Office Work Solution Provider

# gemini response

Money Forward is often called a **Fintech company** and a **SaaS company**.

More specifically, it's known for providing:

* **"Money Forward ME"**: A personal financial management (PFM) application for individuals. It's often described as a **personal finance management application** or a **household account book app**.
* **"Money Forward Cloud"**: A suite of cloud-based services for businesses to optimize their back-office operations. This is often referred to as a **SaaS platform for back-office operations** or a **cloud solution for back office optimization**. This suite includes various tools for accounting, invoicing, payroll, and more.

While you might hear them described in general terms like "a company that offers office work solutions," their more precise and commonly used descriptors are "Fintech company" and "SaaS company." They are also often recognized as a leading player in these areas within Japan.
